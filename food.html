Famous Dishes

Misal Pav

Misal Pav is a popular Maharashtrian street food made with spicy sprouted lentil curry served with bread.

Puran Poli

Puran Poli is a sweet flatbread filled with jaggery and lentils. It is usually prepared during festivals and special occasions.

Shenga Chutney

This is a dry peanut chutney made with roasted peanuts, garlic, and spices. It is often eaten with bhakri or chapati.

Traditional Textile Specialty

Solapur Chaddars

Solapur is famous for its textile products, especially the traditional Solapur chaddars (bed sheets) known for their quality and unique designs.

Local markets and food stalls offer authentic flavors of the region and give visitors a chance to experience the traditional food culture of Solapur.

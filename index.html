About Solapur

Solapur is a historic city in Maharashtra known for its spiritual places, rich traditions, handloom textiles and delicious food. The city attracts tourists and pilgrims from all over India.

Famous places such as Pandharpur, Akkalkot, Siddheshwar Temple and Tuljapur make Solapur an important religious destination.

Welcome to Solapur

Solapur is a historic and culturally rich city located in the southern part of Maharashtra, close to the borders of Karnataka and Telangana. The city is known for its spiritual centers, historic monuments, traditional festivals, and vibrant culture.

For centuries, Solapur has been an important destination for pilgrims and travelers. Famous spiritual places like Siddheshwar Temple, Pandharpur and Akkalkot attract millions of devotees every year.

The city is also famous for Solapur chaddars, traditional festivals and delicious food. Visitors can experience spirituality, culture and local traditions together.
